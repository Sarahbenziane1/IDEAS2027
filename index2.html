About the Conference

What is IDEAS'2027?

A national platform dedicated to advancing AI research, data processing, and intelligent systems — now opening to international collaboration.

Advancing Algeria's AI Ecosystem

The National Conference on Innovation in Data Engineering and AI Science (IDEAS'2027) unites researchers, students, and professionals to exchange ideas, promote collaboration, and shape the future of AI and data science.

Held in Oran at USTO-MB, IDEAS'2027 marks a new milestone as the conference opens to international participation, promoting global collaboration in artificial intelligence and data engineering.

The conference aims to encourage interdisciplinary collaboration between academia and industry, promote responsible and ethical development of AI technologies, and inspire the next generation of researchers and innovators.
