About IDEAS'2027?

The National Conference on Innovation in Data Engineering and AI Science (IDEAS’2027) unites researchers, students, and professionals to exchange ideas, promote collaboration, and shape the future of AI and data science in Oran, Algeria.

  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning

  • Image processing & Pattern Recognition

  • Cloud Computing & Big Data

  • Neural Networks & Intelligent Systems

  • Cybersecurity & Cryptography

  • Internet of Things (IoT)

  • Robotics & Automation

  • Mathematical Modelling & Optimization

Workshops & Tutorials

  • Data Engineering Frameworks and Pipelines

  • Deep Learning Applications in Image and Speech Recognition

  • Responsible and Ethical AI

  • AI Tools for Research and Industry

Proceedings & Publications

All accepted and presented papers at IDEAS’2027 will be included in the IEEE conference proceedings.
Selected outstanding papers will be invited for publication in national scientific journals.

Authors are invited to submit original and unpublished research contributions related to data engineering and artificial intelligence.

  • IEEE and Word templates available online

  • Submissions limited to 6 pages (including references)

  • Review and selection by the Program Committee
